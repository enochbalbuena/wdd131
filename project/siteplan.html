Skincare Guide for Healthy Skin

Site Name

The site name is "Skincare Guide for Healthy Skin". The name was selected because it clearly explains the purpose of the site, which is to provide skincare routines and tips for maintaining healthy skin.

Site Purpose

The site aims to educate visitors on effective skincare routines, provide valuable tips and advice on skincare, and offer a way for visitors to contact us for personalized advice or inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What are the best skincare routines for different skin types?
  • How can I get personalized skincare advice?

Color Schema

  • Primary Background Color: #FFFFFF (White)
  • Secondary Color for Headings and Buttons: #104E8B (Dodger Blue 4)
  • Accent Color for Footer and Links: #191970 (Midnight Blue)
  • Text Color: #000000 (Black)

Typography

The primary font for the body text is 'Open Sans', sans-serif. The font for headings is 'Montserrat', sans-serif.
